The Manifesto Behind prettyfunny.xyz

prettyfunny.xyz started as a personal project back when I was in 7th grade. What began as simple curiosity quickly turned into an obsession with how websites are made.

With nothing more than a GitHub account and a strong Wi-Fi connection, I launched the site completely hosted via GitHub Pages. It was a mix of trial and error, late-night Googling, and sheer determination.

The internet felt like a giant playground, and I was eager to build my own corner of it. Every HTML tag, every CSS tweak—it all felt like magic. And prettyfunny.xyz became the space where I could experiment and share that magic.

This site isn’t just a domain—it's a memory of youthful curiosity, a living experiment, and a place that reminds me why I love building things on the web.

Built with passion, mistakes, learning, and lots of coffee (eventually ☕).

Still hosted 100% on GitHub—because that’s where it all began.
