What is Discord?

Discord is a free voice, video, and text communication app used by millions of people to talk and hang out with their communities and friends. It's used for everything from casual gaming chats to professional workspaces.

Why Discord?

  • Crystal-clear voice channels and group calls
  • Organized servers with channels and roles
  • Low-latency, high-quality voice and video
  • Custom bots and automation
  • Powerful integrations and rich embeds
