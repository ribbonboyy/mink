What is YouTube?

YouTube is the world’s largest video-sharing platform, allowing users to upload, view, comment on, and share videos. From music and tutorials to live streams and personal vlogs, YouTube hosts billions of videos watched by millions daily.

Key Features

  • Video Uploading and Sharing
  • Live Streaming and Premieres
  • Content Monetization via Ads and Memberships
  • Subscriptions and Personalized Feeds
  • Community Features like Posts, Shorts, and Stories

Creators and Content

YouTube empowers creators of all backgrounds. Whether you're a filmmaker, gamer, educator, or musician, the platform gives you the tools to grow an audience and turn your passion into a profession.

Learning and Entertainment

YouTube isn’t just for entertainment. It’s a powerful tool for learning. Thousands of educational channels cover topics from science and math to art and language learning—often for free.

Why People Love YouTube

YouTube offers a personalized experience with recommendations based on your watch history. Its vast content library, intuitive interface, and global accessibility make it a go-to source for content of all kinds.

Get Started

To use YouTube, simply visit the site or app. You can watch without an account, but signing in lets you like, comment, subscribe, and create your own videos.
