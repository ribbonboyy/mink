Why Czechia is Amazing

  • 🏰 Home to over 2,000 castles and chateaux – more than any other country in Europe!
  • 🍺 The birthplace of Pilsner beer and the world's top beer consumer per capita.
  • 🎓 High literacy rate (99%) and excellent education system.
  • 🌳 One of the greenest countries in Europe with 33% forest coverage.

Cultural Highlights

  • 🎶 Famous music festivals: Prague Spring Festival and Colours of Ostrava.
  • 🥟 Iconic foods: Svíčková, Trdelník, and the world-famous Pilsner beer.
  • 🎨 Rich artistic legacy from Alfons Mucha to Franz Kafka.
  • 🎭 Unique folk traditions, including colorful Moravian costumes and dances.
