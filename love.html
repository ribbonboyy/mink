Love and Affection

Love and affection are fundamental human emotions and behaviors that foster deep interpersonal bonds and emotional support. They are crucial for emotional well-being and social connections.

Brief Definitions

  • Love: An intense feeling of deep affection, attachment, and care for someone or something.
  • Affection: A gentle feeling of fondness or liking often expressed through actions like hugging, smiling, or verbal affirmations.
  • Compassion: Sympathetic consciousness of others' distress together with a desire to alleviate it.
  • Empathy: The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.
