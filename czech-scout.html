Junák – Czech Scouting Association

Junák – Czech Scouting is the largest youth organization in the Czech Republic, empowering young people through outdoor adventures, leadership development, and community service based on Scouting values.

Who We Are

Founded in 1911, Junák has played a vital role in the history and personal development of young Czechs. The movement combines traditional Scouting values with modern approaches to education and leadership training. Through practical experiences in nature, teamwork, and service, Scouts gain valuable life skills and build lifelong friendships.

  • Over 70,000 active members across the country
  • Official member of WOSM (World Organization of the Scout Movement) and WAGGGS (World Association of Girl Guides and Girl Scouts)
  • Active in both local communities and international cooperation
  • Operates with full gender inclusivity – boys and girls work together

Our Mission & Values

We believe in raising responsible, self-confident, and kind individuals who are ready to contribute to society. The Scout Law and Promise are at the core of every activity we do. We encourage members to be brave, loyal, trustworthy, helpful, and to live sustainably with nature.

  • A Scout is helpful to others and contributes to their community
  • A Scout respects truth, justice, and integrity
  • A Scout protects the environment and lives in harmony with it
  • A Scout is courageous, cheerful, and resourceful in all situations

Organizational Structure

Junák operates through a structured system of local groups, units (called "oddíly"), and patrols. This framework allows Scouts to experience personal progression and take on leadership roles gradually, often starting from young Cub Scouts all the way to leading teams and organizing camps as Rovers and adult volunteers.

Each unit organizes regular weekly meetings, weekend trips, and large-scale summer camps. The camps are a highlight of the year, allowing Scouts to live in nature for two or three weeks, cook over a fire, learn traditional skills, and bond as a team.

International Participation

Junák proudly represents Czech Scouting at international events such as World Scout Jamborees, European Scout Conferences, and partner exchanges with other national organizations. These opportunities offer cultural exchange, broaden perspectives, and strengthen global peace and friendship through shared Scouting values.
