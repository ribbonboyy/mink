---------------------------------------------------------------
🚀 **International Space Station (ISS)**: 408 km above Earth, it’s like a permanent floating laboratory.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🌌 **Low Earth Orbit**: This is where most satellites orbit – perfect for GPS and weather tracking!
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🔭 **Hubble Space Telescope**: Capturing cosmic wonders since 1990. It’s located at 547 km altitude.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🌍 **Kármán Line**: The edge of space begins at 100 km! Below this, airplanes work; above, only rockets do.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
✨ **Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights)**: Happens between 80-640 km when solar particles meet our atmosphere.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
☄️ **Meteors Burn Up Here**: The Mesosphere (50-80 km) is where shooting stars light up the night sky.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🦅 **Highest Bird Flight**: The Rüppell's vulture reached an incredible 11.3 km altitude, dodging planes!
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🏔 **Mount Everest**: At 8.8 km, it’s the tallest mountain on Earth, yet dwarfed by the scale of our journey!
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🌪 **Troposphere**: Up to 20 km high, this layer contains all the weather, clouds, and most of Earth’s oxygen.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🪂 **Felix Baumgartner's Jump**: In 2012, he jumped from 39 km and broke the sound barrier during freefall.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🌋 **Mauna Loa**: The world's largest volcano rises 4.1 km above sea level and stretches much farther underwater.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🚁 **Highest Helicopter Flight**: 12.4 km in 1972 – at this altitude, the air is too thin for most aircraft.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🌈 **Rainbows Can't Exist Here**: Beyond 20 km, the air is too thin for light to refract the way it does in rainbows.
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🚢 **Titanic Wreck Site**: The Titanic rests at a depth of 3.8 km. Imagine flipping this depth vertically!
---------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------
🌍 **Earth’s Surface**: Finally back! You’ve traveled through 408 km of space to Earth. What a ride!
